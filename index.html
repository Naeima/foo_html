Ontology Metadata

  • Version IRI: https://w3id.org/def/foo#V2.0
  • Abstract: The Forest Observatory Ontology (FOO) comprises a novel upper-level ontology that integrates wildlife data generated by sensors. FOO borrows classes and properties from SOSA and BBC wildlife ontology.
  • Description: This ontology describes wildlife observations generated by sensors.

Classes

  • Sensor: A device that observes or measures a physical property and records, indicates, or otherwise responds to it. (rdf:type sosa:Sensor; rdfs:label "Sensor"@en.)
  • Observation: An act of monitoring or measuring an environmental or physical property. (rdf:type sosa:Observation; rdfs:label "Observation"@en.)
  • Observable Property: A property that can be observed. (rdf:type sosa:ObservableProperty; rdfs:label "Observable Property"@en.)
  • Feature of Interest: A feature that is the target of observation. (rdf:type sosa:FeatureOfInterest; rdfs:label "Feature of Interest"@en.)
  • Elephas Maximus: An elephant species. (rdf:type wo:Family/Elephantidae; rdfs:label "Elephas Maximus")
